The Department of Commerce is a vital part of the academic sector, offering students the opportunity to gain knowledge and skills in various areas of business and commerce. This department typically offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in subjects such as accounting, finance, marketing, economics and business law.

The curriculum of the Department of Commerce is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the business world, including the latest trends, practices, and technologies. Students also have the opportunity to gain practical experience through internships and projects, which can help them develop important skills that are highly valued by employers.

The Department of Commerce is often closely linked with the business community, and many academic programs in this department have strong industry connections. This means that students may have access to guest lectures, networking events, and job opportunities through these connections.
