Welcome to SAHYAJYOTHI

DREAM BIG, AIM HIGH, THINK SAHYAJYOTHI

A Catholic Institution, Aiming at Education since 2014

OUR MISSION

We, in Sahyajyothi strive to ensure essential skills for a successful career-intellectual competence, inquisitiveness and critical thinking; to teach true human values that would make learners charitable, hardworking and disciplined human beings; to build strength of character, self-respect, self-confidence and far sightedness and to provide necessary support and encourage -ment to the weak and the marginalized people of rural regions. All these objectives and goals are based on the love of God and service to mankind as revealed by Jesus Christ so as to send the intellectually retightened and minds to make a distinctive difference in the society for nation-building.

OUR VISION

We envision to transform this institution to a premium institution of higher learning and a pinnacle of excellence and to mould the youth of the country into selfless citizens of our great democracy. Our attempts are to evolve good human beings, competent professionals and people who lead a successful and joyful life. Our efforts are to lead persons in faith, tolerance and compassion and to open up the path to attain integrity and human wholeness the impacting of the knowledge is thoroughly through a holistic catholic education process.

Affiliation

Sahyajyothi College is affiliated with Mahatma Gandhi University, which is a state university located in Kottayam, Kerala, India.

Location

Sahyajyothi College is located in Kumily, which is a town in the Idukki district of Kerala, India.

Faculty

We have a dedicated and experienced faculty team who are committed to providing high-quality education to their students.

Extra-Curricular Activities

The college has facilities for outdoor sports such as basketball and badminton. The cultural events include festivals, music and dance competitions.

First & Best

Sahyajyothi is the first and best Arts and Science college in Idukki.

Learn to live

Rather than teaching syllabus to students, Sahyajyothi disseminates the art of living to their students so as to ensure that every parent will get a good human in their children rather than getting a simple degree holder..

Features

Quality education and excellent academic standards.

Sahyajyothi Arts and Science College is known for providing quality education to students. The college has highly qualified faculty members who are experts in their respective fields. They use innovative teaching methods and technology to provide students with a stimulating learning environment. The college has also implemented a continuous evaluation system to assess the performance of students and provide them with feedback and support to improve their academic performance.

Modern infrastructure and facilities

The college has modern infrastructure and facilities to provide students with a comfortable and conducive learning environment. The college has spacious classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a well-stocked library, and modern computer labs. The college also provides various sports facilities, including a gymnasium, football ground, basketball court, and volleyball court.

Career guidance and placement assistance

Sahyajyothi Arts and Science College has a dedicated placement cell that provides career guidance and placement assistance to students. The cell conducts training sessions, workshops, and seminars to prepare students for job interviews and recruitments. The college has a good track record of placements, with many of its students being recruited by top companies in India and abroad.

Holistic development and extracurricular activities

Sahyajyothi Arts and Science College believes in the overall development of students and encourages them to participate in co-curricular and extracurricular activities. The college provides various opportunities for students to showcase their talents and develop their skills. The college organizes various cultural events, seminars, workshops, and sports competitions that help students develop their leadership, teamwork, and communication skills. Additionally, the college encourages students to participate in various community service initiatives to promote social responsibility and civic engagement.
