Department of Malayalam (Allied)

Department information

  • Department: Malayalam (Allied)
  • H.O.D: Dr. Kavitha K.

The Department of Malayalam is an allied academic department that offer programs in the field of humanities and few other departments. This department typically focuses on the study of Malayalam language, literature, and culture. Students who enroll in this department can expect to learn about the history, evolution, and rich cultural heritage of Kerala, as well as the development of Malayalam literature over the years.
